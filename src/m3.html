Milestone 3

Process

After completing our contextual inquiry, we compiled our notes to analyze distinct archetypes among the users we observed. Among all user types we found that a set of three characteristic traits defined their usage patterns. The three traits we identified are as follows: pet ownership experience, search specificity, and the Amount of effort the user is willing to spend in caring for or training their pet. We represent these characteristic traits on our personas as scale rankings and referred to these scales as a key way to define our personas.

Through this process we identified our primary persona as the New Pet Owner, Kristina. Although Kristanaâ€™s scale rankings are unique to her, the goals and frustrations of Kristina encompassed those of all other user personas. Our three secondary personas were the Existing Pet Owner who is looking to add an additional pet to their household, the Previous Pet Owner who owned pets in the past but currently does not own a pet that they need to accommodate for in their search, and lastly the 3rd Party Adopter that is looking for a pet for someone else.
